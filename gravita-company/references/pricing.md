# Gravita — Pricing & Billing

## Project Pricing Model

Gravita charges **per week**, not per hour. The weekly rate is determined by the value we potentially provide the customer, identified during the scoping call.

### Weekly Rates

| Client Type | Weekly Rate |
|-------------|-------------|
| Medium-sized companies | £5,000 – £7,000 |
| Enterprise | £7,000 – £12,000 |

### How We Calculate Project Cost

1. Build the roadmap (number of weeks)
2. Multiply weeks × weekly rate
3. Rate is set based on the value we identify at scoping

### Invoicing Terms

- Invoices are paid **upfront for each week**
- At the end of each week, if the customer is happy, we proceed to the next week and invoice
- Contracts can be extended by adding extra weeks
- Payment terms: typically **14 days**, maximum **30 days** when requested
- No long-term lock-in — the weekly cadence means either side can pause

### Red Flags to Warn Prospects About
When prospects are comparing quotes, flag:
- **Suspiciously low quotes:** Often means junior team, offshore handoff, or template-based work
- **Excessively high quotes:** May indicate bloated process, unnecessary phases, or agency overhead
- **Time estimates that seem too fast or too slow** for the scope

## Proposal Template Notes

When writing a proposal:
- Lead with the business problem and desired outcomes, not deliverables
- Show the weekly roadmap with clear milestones
- State the weekly rate and total estimate
- Include the benchmarking advice (compare us, watch for red flags)
- Close with next steps — typically a discovery workshop booking
