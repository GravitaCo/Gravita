# Gravita — Client Engagement Model

## Who We Work With

### Ideal Client Profile
- Medium-sized companies or enterprise
- Teams who value narrative and design process
- Companies wanting to set industry standards, not follow
- Leaders who want their brand to stand out
- Teams that need autonomy — they want systems, not ongoing dependency
- No egos — collaborative, outcome-driven stakeholders

### Client Engagement Signals (Good Fit)
- They ask about outcomes and ROI, not just deliverables
- They involve decision-makers in the scoping call
- They're willing to invest in discovery before jumping to design
- They see the website / brand as a business asset, not a cost center
- They communicate openly and frequently

## Engagement Lifecycle

### 1. Initial Contact
- Prospect reaches out or we do outreach
- Goal: understand if there's a fit before investing time

### 2. Scoping Call
- Deep-dive into their challenges (short and long term)
- Business goals beyond "keeping up with trends"
- ROI expectations
- We explain our process, systems, and collaborative approach
- See `process.md` for the full scoping question framework

### 3. Roadmap & Proposal
- Weekly breakdown of work and costs
- Sent as a benchmark tool for the prospect
- Red flag warnings included

### 4. Discovery Workshop
- Half-day to full-day
- Gather all collateral and context
- Produce comprehensive documentation (see `process.md`)

### 5. Design & Build
- Weekly cadence with upfront invoicing
- Daily communication via Slack or Teams
- All stakeholders have visibility

### 6. Launch & Handover
- Site goes live / brand platform delivered
- Design system handover with documentation
- Client has full autonomy to operate

## Communication Standards

- **Frequency:** Daily or near-daily updates during active project phases
- **Channels:** Slack or Microsoft Teams (client preference)
- **Visibility:** Every stakeholder on the project sees progress — not just the primary contact
- **Format:** Short, clear updates. No lengthy status reports unless requested.
