# Gravita — Company Overview

## What Gravita Is

Gravita (gravita.co) is a design and web agency that builds brand platforms, marketing websites, and design systems. We work hands-on in Webflow and Figma, delivering production-ready outputs — not just mockups or strategy decks.

We are not a traditional agency. We don't do long pitches, spec work, or bloated timelines. We embed with client teams, communicate daily, and ship weekly.

## What We Do

- **Brand Platforms & Narrative:** Develop the strategic story behind a brand — mission, values, purpose, traits, culture — and bring it to life across every touchpoint.
- **Websites:** Design and build marketing websites in Webflow. Lead generation, brand presence, product marketing — scoped to real business goals.
- **Design Systems:** Build component-based design systems in Figma and Webflow that enable rapid, compliant, accessible production.
- **Value Proposition Development:** Help clients articulate real business outcomes — not just benefits or brand values. (See process.md for the full framework.)

## Who We Work With

We work with teams who:
- Value the narratives we build and the design process
- Want to set industry standards, not follow trends
- Want their brand to stand out from the crowd
- Need autonomy — they want systems, not ongoing dependency on us

Our clients span industries including financial services, defense tech, hospitality, construction, and more. We typically work with medium-sized companies and enterprise.

## Our Differentiators

1. **Autonomy over dependency:** We build systems (brand platforms, design systems, Webflow sites) so clients can move fast without us.
2. **Daily communication:** Slack or Teams, daily or near-daily updates. Full visibility for all stakeholders.
3. **Practitioner-led:** We design and build. There's no handoff to a junior team or offshore partner.
4. **Value-driven scoping:** We don't quote hours. We scope by weeks, priced against the value we deliver.
5. **Speed to market:** Our design system and Webflow approach means rapid production and deployment.
