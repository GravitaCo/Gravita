# Gravita — Voice & Tone Guide

## Brand Voice

Gravita's voice is **confident, direct, and collaborative**. We speak as builders — people who design and ship, not consultants who theorize from the sidelines.

### Voice Attributes

| Attribute | What It Means | What It Doesn't Mean |
|-----------|--------------|---------------------|
| **Confident** | We know our craft and stand behind our process | Arrogant, dismissive, or condescending |
| **Direct** | We say what we mean without filler or fluff | Blunt, cold, or impersonal |
| **Collaborative** | We work alongside clients, not above them | Passive, deferential, or wishy-washy |
| **Honest** | We flag risks, set real expectations, and call out red flags | Negative, pessimistic, or fear-mongering |
| **Outcome-focused** | We talk in terms of business impact | Jargon-heavy, vague, or feature-obsessed |

### Tone Shifts by Context

| Context | Lean Toward |
|---------|------------|
| Proposals & outreach | Confident + outcome-focused. Lead with impact. |
| Client updates / Slack | Collaborative + direct. Quick, clear, no fluff. |
| Marketing copy (website) | Confident + honest. Set us apart from generic agencies. |
| Case studies | Outcome-focused + direct. Numbers and results, not process bragging. |
| Support / issue resolution | Collaborative + honest. Acknowledge, fix, communicate. |

## Writing Principles

### 1. Push to Value Propositions
Never stop at benefits. Always reach for the business outcome.

**Weak:** "We build beautiful websites that convert."
**Better:** "We build brand platforms that reduce client dependency on agencies and accelerate time-to-market by weeks."

**Weak:** "Our design systems ensure brand consistency."
**Better:** "Our design systems give your team the autonomy to ship compliant, accessible pages without waiting on a designer."

### 2. No Jargon Without Purpose
If a term doesn't add clarity, cut it. We don't use buzzwords to sound smart.

**Avoid:** "We leverage synergistic design paradigms to optimize digital touchpoints."
**Use:** "We build design systems that let your team move fast."

### 3. Speak to Decision-Makers
Our audience is founders, heads of marketing, and product leads. They value:
- Clarity over decoration
- Speed over process
- Outcomes over deliverables
- Honesty over salesmanship

### 4. Show, Don't Claim
Instead of saying "we're the best," demonstrate it through specifics.

**Weak:** "We're a world-class design agency."
**Better:** "We built [client]'s brand platform in 6 weeks. They launched their Series B site the same month."

### 5. No Ego
We never put ourselves above the client's team. The work is collaborative. Credit is shared. The measure of success is the client's outcome, not our portfolio.

## Phrases We Use

- "Brand platform" (not "branding package")
- "Design system" (not "style guide" — a design system is functional, not just documentation)
- "Value proposition" (not "benefits" or "brand value" — see process.md for the distinction)
- "Speed to market"
- "Autonomy" / "remove resource dependencies"
- "Weekly cadence"
- "Stakeholder visibility"

## Phrases We Avoid

- "Leverage" / "synergy" / "optimize" (unless used precisely)
- "Best-in-class" / "world-class" / "cutting-edge"
- "Digital transformation"
- "Bespoke" (overused in UK agency space)
- "Solutions" (vague — say what it actually is)
- "Touch base" / "circle back" (corporate filler)

## Proposal & Outreach Voice

When writing proposals or outreach for Gravita:

1. **Open with their problem**, not our credentials
2. **Name the business impact** of solving it
3. **Describe the approach** in plain language — weekly roadmap, daily communication, no surprises
4. **Close with a clear next step** — usually booking a scoping call or discovery workshop
5. **Include the benchmarking advice** — "Use our roadmap to compare anyone else you're talking to"

### Example Outreach Opener (Adapt, Don't Copy)
"Most agencies will sell you a website. We'd rather understand what a successful next 12 months looks like for your team, and work backward from there. If lead generation is the goal, that shapes everything — from sitemap to CMS structure to how we measure success."
