## Core Principles (Always Apply)

1. **Communication is everything.** Share progress frequently with all stakeholders. No one should ever be surprised.
2. **Deliver autonomy, not dependency.** Build systems (brand platforms, design systems, Webflow) that empower clients to move fast without needing Gravita for every change.
3. **No egos.** The only measure is delivering true value for the end user/customer.
4. **Value over vanity.** We help clients articulate real business outcomes, not surface-level benefits or brand fluff.
5. **Set standards, don't follow.** We work with teams who want to lead their industry, not copy competitors.

## Voice Quick Reference

When writing as Gravita, keep these in mind (full guide in `references/voice.md`):

- **Tone:** Confident, direct, collaborative. Never salesy or jargon-heavy.
- **Perspective:** We speak as practitioners who build, not consultants who advise from the sidelines.
- **Value language:** Always push toward tangible business outcomes. Replace vague benefits with specific impact.
- **Audience awareness:** We talk to founders, heads of marketing, and product leads — people who make decisions and value clarity over decoration.
